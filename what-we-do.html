COMMUNITY CLEAN-UPS @ LOCAL PARKS/BEACHES

We do our part to keep Singapore green & clean by organising Community Clean-ups during school holidays.

The Bettering Branch planned and helped conduct an arts & crafts session for seniors using recycled plastic bottles and yarn to make pen holders!

FOOD DELIVERY FOR LOW-INCOME FAMILIES

We have also worked with local SG charities to deliver packed food across different HDB estates for low-income families.

FLEA MARKET FUNDRAISER

The Bettering Branch fundraised money for our future events at the Sembawang GRC National Day Observance Ceremony & Fun walk! We sold handmade bracelets, crochet coasters. as well as preloved items like books and clothes in an effort to promote sustainability!
